# Product Brief: What's That Thing?

## Summary

What's That Thing? is a lightweight local-first prototype for remembering the exact products, supplies, replacements, refills, and restocks a person buys or uses. It helps a user answer: what is the exact brand, flavor, size, model number, usual store, backup store, and when should I check whether I need more?

## MVP Goals

- Provide a fast way to capture a thing with category, exact product details, usual store or location, notes, check-stock date, and stock status.
- Separate common home contexts into Pets, Household, Personal, and Plants.
- Surface upcoming stock checks without requiring a calendar integration.
- Generate a simple shopping list from low, out-of-stock, or manually added products and supplies.
- Keep the prototype local-only and dependency-free.

## Primary User

A person who repeatedly forgets the exact food, litter, filter, bulb, toiletry, coffee, supplement, soil, plant food, or replacement part they meant to buy again.

## Core Views

- Pets: cat food brand/flavor/size, litter brand/type/size, reptile substrate brand/type/amount, aquarium filter model/media, and pet supplies.
- Household: fridge filter model number, furnace or air filter size, light bulb wattage/base/style, batteries, cleaning products, parts, and replacement supplies.
- Personal: shampoo, deodorant, razors, coffee roast, protein bar flavor, toiletries, and personal staples.
- Plants: plant food, soil, pot size, grow light model, and plant supplies.
- Add Thing: quick capture form for new records.
- Reminders: stock checks and replacement checks due within the next 14 days.
- Details: selected item notes, metadata, and actions.
- Shopping List: low, out-of-stock, or manually added supplies.

## Non-Goals For MVP

- Accounts, sync, multi-device storage, or collaboration.
- Push notifications or external calendar integration.
- Barcode scanning, image uploads, OCR, or AI identification.
- Purchase links or retailer integrations.
- Plant watering, pet feeding schedules, litter box cleaning, substrate changes as chores, repotting reminders, plant care advice, household chore tracking, or recommendations about what care should be done.

## Success Criteria

- A user can add a thing in under one minute.
- A user can find an item by category or search.
- A user can see which products or supplies may need a stock check soon.
- A user can maintain a shopping list without leaving the page.
